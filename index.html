What We Do

Ineqaulities abound in the world, it is our aim to give a chance via education to the least of us. The basic foundations of life elude a lot of us by virtue of our birth. We focus on reigniting that can do spirit in all of us. Please join us as we take a step at a time

The Dooyum Dio Foundation is a non profit organisation based in Kornya, Konshisha local government, Benue state Nigeria

Who We Are

Dooyum Dio Foundation is a community development organization founded in 2013 by Ernest Dio. The Foundation is a not for profit Non Governmental Organization which seeks to advance and espouse the cause of the Nigerian youth with the objective of assisting them attain their potential.

Mission

  • To empower the youth for a more meaningful tomorrow
  • To assist the destitute attain a better living standard
  • To provide personal development for our youth
  • To provide a conducive and competitive environment for educational advancement

Scholarship

We organise annual academic competitions and the winner is awarded a fully funded scholarship to any university in Nigeria.The winner also has his/her external examinations fully registered by the Dooyum Dio Foundation as well as the remaining term tuition paid in full.
